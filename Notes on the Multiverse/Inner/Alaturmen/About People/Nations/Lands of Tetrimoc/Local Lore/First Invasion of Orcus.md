# The Invasion of Orcus
## Written by [[Poppy Bumbleheart]]

In the eve of the first day of Still, in the 87th year after Tetrimoc had Fallen, a hum broke the sky. And all Orcus could do was smile.

Over the course of the following month, many dozens of magicians and scientists formed hypotheses about what this event was or meant, but only the Grey Ordeal knew, as the Old Man had warned only them. They traveled to the Deserts of Tetrimoc, only a half-week's ride by horseback from their Keep in Arthur's Prairie, meeting the Yellow Mage of the Guild of Wizards, in a dark and damp cave many miles north of the Golden City. His distain for his surroundings was visibly apparent in his posture and facial expression.
