## Outlander Nomad

You have spent your life before adventure roaming [[The Deserts of Tetrimoc]], hunting and raiding to survive. You understand that all meat is meat, and all creatures must eat. 

You gain +2 to your Constitution Score and +1 to your Dexterity Score. You are proficient in the Perception and Insight Skills. You know [[Infernal (language)]] and [[Desertspeech (language)]].

In addition, you gain the benefits of the Ranger's Natural Explorer feature while you are traveling in a desert.

## Gnoll

![[gnol.png]]

People with a heavy Gnoll heritage find themselves in possession of a very canaine biology and a nearly insatiable appetite, both for food and other vices. The origins of Gnoll people on Alaturmen are tied directly to the [[First Invasion of Orcus]].

- *Age* You may live up to 75 years.
- *Weight* You may weigh anywhere from 140 to 240 pounds.
- You may be anywhere from 5 to 7 feet tall. Your size is Medium.
- Your base walking speed is 35 feet per round.
- When you hit with an unarmed melee attack, you may add an additional 1d4 slashing damage to the normal damage of the attack.
- When you reduce a creature to 0 hit points with a melee attack on your turn, you may use a bonus action to move up to half your speed and make an unarmed melee attack.
